Project Information

This project was designed to solve easy crossword puzzles. It can solve two kinds: First, it can use a thesaurus to solve vocabulary puzzles. The clues for these puzzles need to be synonyms of the answers. Second, when the API cooperates, it can solve newspaper crossword puzzles as long as the clues are not interconnected or themed. This feature is in beta and success depends on the strength of the databases being used. Currently, those databases are CrosswordNexus.com and Dictionary.com. If two or more clue answers are missing from the database, the algorithm is unable to find a solution, so it's best to stick to smaller or easier puzzles, those occurring earlier in the week.

The program uses constraint-satisfaction problem algorithms, such as forward-checking and backtracking, to select proper-fitting answers out of the potential guesses. In the case of synonym puzzles, some of the answers may not seem to be a perfect fit, but it's important to remember that words can have a variety of meanings depending on how and it what time period they are used.
