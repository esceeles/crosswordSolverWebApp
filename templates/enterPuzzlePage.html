Crossword Solver

This is a crossword solver application. It can solve synonym/vocab crossword puzzles or more complicated ones. It can't solve puzzles with clues that reference eachother.

First enter the array dimensions, followed by a blank line, then list the across clues and down clues in "number:clue" format, as seen in the sample puzzle. The last part is the grid. Black squares are '#', numbered squares are just the number, and empty squares are '_'.
